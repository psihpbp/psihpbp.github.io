Sustainability Consultation
Launched in 2016, the Hyde Park Business Partnership is a program within the University of Chicagoâ€™s Phoenix Sustainability Initiative that focuses on providing green consulting services. Having gained momentum and visibility within the University campus cafes, we are expanding our activities into the Hyde Park neighborhood, fostering a more direct connection between the University and its Hyde Park home. Within this program, we seek to provide cost-effective, resource saving strategies and practices with the resources to save money, positively impact the environment, and appeal to consumers.

Our core initiatives are to:

INFORM

businesses of existing sustainability measures and the benefits of implementing them

DESIGN

strategies for our partnered businesses based on:

  • business sustainability values
  • sustainability certification targets
  • available time and financial resources
  • accessible information on energy, water, and gas usage

IMPROVE

the relationship between businesses and the environment by shifting businesses to more sustainable practices
