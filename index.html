Mission

The PSI Hyde Park Business Partnership is a pro bono consulting goup that aims to promote sustainability in local Hyde Park Businesses. We offer consulting services, from detailing a step-by-step plan for your business to improve sustainability and acquire an environmental certificate, to general practices your business can implement.

Why it Matters

For the Environment

Cumulative negligent business practice can cause lasting detrimental effects on the environment.

  • According to the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency, industrial and commercial energy use (from such sources as electricity use, product transportation, industrial processes, burning fossil fuels to power boilers and produce steam, and using gasoline to power vehicles) accounts for nearly 30 percent of total U.S. greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Heating and air conditioning systems pump greenhouse gas emissions from offices into the atmosphere and use up vast amounts of electricity. Many buildings aren’t designed to include energy-efficient systems or technology to reduce the amount of heat and air conditioning they use.
  • According to the Green Restaurant Association, the average restaurant wastes between 25,000 and 75,000 pounds of food every year.

For your Business

“Green marketing refers to the process of selling products and/or services based on their environmental benefits.”

Conducting a comprehensive waste audit can help identify process inefficiencies and help to significantly reduce operating costs.

  • Being green can be a part of your brand’s identity. According to the Natural Marketing Institute, consumers are 58% more likely to choose brands that value sustainability.
  • Brand loyalty is no only a matter of the best product at the best price. Instead, it’s a matter of demonstrating to consumers that your brand is more than a brand but also an embodiment of the ideals that they hold most important.

Who We Are

We are Phoenix Sustainability Initiative. We are a student organization that seeks to actively share and develop environmentally sustainable practices and policies within the UChicago and Hyde Park communities.

Through close communication with the University staff, students, and partner organizations, we execute a variety of student-sourced projects, ranging from environmentally-focused community service to connecting with sustainability-minded businesses in the city for student careers programming.
